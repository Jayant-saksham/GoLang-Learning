gRPC (gRPC Remote Procedure Call) is an open-source remote procedure call (RPC) framework developed by Google. 
It is designed to facilitate communication between distributed systems, allowing them to make remote procedure calls as 
if they were local calls, regardless of the programming languages and platforms used by the systems.
